Visualize Your Tones

The Compare page is meant to look at a native speaker's pronunciation of Mandarin Chinese tones. The record page is used to record yourself. When you play back the recording, a visualizaiton will display your audio, showing where you're changing your tone.

How it Works

As a non-native speaker of tonal languages, it can be hard to understand how tones work, and how to correctly pronounce the right tone. This website serves as a visual aid to language learners who need to practice their tones.
